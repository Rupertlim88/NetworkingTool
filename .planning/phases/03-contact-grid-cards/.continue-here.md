- Three-layer shadows globally: tight (2px), medium (8px), wide ambient (20px)
- Border is responsive via clamp(1.5px, 0.15vw, 3px) — scales with card size
- Border color: solid cyan (`--color-accent-secondary`) per theme
- No glow at rest — glow only appears on hover (stronger contrast, cleaner grid)
- No pulse at rest — hover is the moment of life
- Hover animation: glow surge + sparks/crackle + stars ignite → settles to steady glow
- Stars ignite slightly after border surge (~200ms delay, like electricity traveling down)
- Star halo color matches star semantic color (yellow or blue)
- Click: electric discharge burst, star color may tint the burst
- Mouse leave: smooth fade out
- Border gradient attempts FAILED due to semi-transparent card surfaces — tried ::before, ::after, CSS mask, padding approach — all caused cyan bleed-through. Reverted to simple solid border.
- User still wants border to feel cohesive with 3D card — needs fresh approach next session
